Welcome to smsmv.com

SMSMV is a leading SMS messaging service provider offering two-way SMS communication services from your internet enabled computer. The BulkSMS gateway reaches across borders and connects to over 800 mobile network operators world-wide.

About Us

SMSmv was initially started in 2003 by NATTU, due to the demand for a free SMS provider to text Maldives from aboard. That time it was on smsmv.tk, a free domain from DOT TK and used Dhiraagu free websms account as a carrier. Due to the high traffic and misuses they changed their service to register under a mobile number and limited the daily free SMS to 10. Since that time we affiliated with a bulk SMS provider and continued our free SMS service under smsmv.com. Unfortunately our provider didn’t survive so long, so we had to stop the free web SMS service then. In 2008 we partnered with world class SMS providers and started our API testing and business at a very small scale


Today we have the coverage over 500 global networks and our platform assure you the reliability and stability as we have carrier grade connection to over 26 international SMC’s


Our Mission is to provide the Quality SMS services at an affordable price. We serve to individuals, businesses, NGO as well the Government Authorities.
